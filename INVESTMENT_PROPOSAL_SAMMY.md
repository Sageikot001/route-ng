## Company Overview

### What is Route.ng?

Route.ng is a digital platform that facilitates Apple Gift Card arbitrage transactions. Our platform serves as an intermediary between:

1. **iOS Users (Partners):** Individuals who purchase Apple Gift Cards using their bank accounts
2. **Managers:** Team leaders who recruit, train, and oversee iOS users
3. **Administration:** Our team that verifies transactions, manages payouts, and maintains platform operations

### How It Currently Works

1. Partners receive funding to purchase Apple Gift Cards
2. Partners buy cards through their registered bank accounts
3. Redemption codes are verified through our automated system
4. Partners earn a commission per successful transaction
5. Route.ng earns margin on the cross-border exchange differential

### Our Improved Model (Refund System)

To protect against losses from scammers and dishonest users, we are transitioning to a **Refund System**:

1. Partners use their own funds to purchase Apple Gift Cards
2. Partners submit redemption codes through our platform
3. Codes are verified through our automated system
4. Upon verification, partners receive **full refund + commission** within hours
5. Route.ng earns margin on the international exchange differential

**Why Refund Over Funding:**
- Eliminates risk of partners absconding with funding
- Partners have skin in the game, ensuring genuine transactions
- Faster scaling without capital tied up in trust-building periods
- Attracts serious, committed partners only

Our profit comes from the **transaction spread between Nigerian gift card purchases and international market redemption**. After breaking it down to pay partners and operational costs, we are still able to provide **5% monthly returns** for our investors.

The international demand for Nigerian-sourced gift cards, combined with favorable exchange rates, creates a sustainable margin that benefits all stakeholders:
- Partners earn daily commissions
